What This Document Covers

This document is a technical and functional overview of the Stix & Pebbles e-commerce platform — currently in development at stixandpebbles.pages.dev and launching at stixandpebbles.com. It explains what the platform does, how it's built, and what makes it different from a standard Shopify or WooCommerce store.

Inside you'll find: the complete e-commerce system including product catalogue, persistent cart, and Stripe checkout; the custom AU Post shipping calculator built from real rate tables across 5 international zones; the "Mini Mel" AI shopping assistant that knows every product and its story; the admin dashboard for managing orders, subscribers, content, and analytics; and the security and SEO architecture underneath it all.

For business readers: this shows what a modern online shop looks like when it's built without platform fees, plugin subscriptions, or percentage cuts on every sale. For technical readers: it covers the architecture from Astro SSR through to Stripe Elements tokenisation, weight-based shipping logic, and Workers AI inference with cart-aware context.

01 — What The Platform Does

A complete online shop — not a template with a buy button.

Stix & Pebbles is a full e-commerce platform for Melinda — an artisan making jewellery, ornaments, and art from natural materials found in Far North Queensland. Each piece has a story tied to where its materials came from, and the site feels like browsing an artist's workshop rather than a warehouse.

What Customers Can Do

  • Browse and buy one-of-a-kind handmade pieces
  • Chat with "Mini Mel" — an AI that knows every product
  • Filter by category: Wearables, Bling, Ornaments, Collections
  • Get accurate shipping estimates to anywhere in the world
  • Sign up for newsletters about new pieces
  • Place custom orders for bespoke commissions

What Melinda Can Do (Admin)

  • View messages with AI-suggested replies in her voice
  • Manage newsletter subscribers with double opt-in
  • See analytics on customer questions and patterns
  • Generate blog content with AI assistance
  • Track orders with full customer and shipping details
  • Manage product stock, pricing, and descriptions

02 — E-Commerce Platform

Complete shopping experience. No Shopify. No platform fees.

Every product gets a description, price, materials list, weight (for shipping calculations), and a meaning — the story behind it. Stock is tracked automatically. The cart persists across browser sessions so customers don't lose their selections.

Product Catalogue

Images, descriptions, materials, meanings, stock management for every piece.

Persistent Cart

localStorage — live item count, quantity controls. Survives browser sessions.

Stripe Checkout

PCI compliant — card details never touch the server. Tokenised directly to Stripe.

Order Management

Server-side validation, database storage, confirmation emails, admin order tracking.

Custom Orders

Bespoke commissions page for customers who want personalised pieces.

AU Post Shipping Calculator

A proper logistics tool. Every item has a weight in grams. At checkout, the system adds cart weights plus a packaging allowance, then calculates the correct rate from real Australia Post rate tables across five international zones.

Zone Coverage Rate Type
1–2Domestic Australia (Standard + Express)Exact AU Post rates
3New ZealandBase + per-kg calculation
4Asia-PacificBase + per-kg calculation
5US / UK / Europe / Rest of WorldBase + per-kg calculation

Built from real rate tables — no third-party plugin, no subscription, no external API.

03 — Mini Mel — The AI Persona

Warm, conversational, knows every product and its story.

Mini Mel is an AI shopping assistant built on Workers AI that helps visitors find the right gift, explains the significance of creek crystals, or describes what makes a particular wind chime resonate. It speaks in Melinda's voice — warm and personal, not corporate. Proactive triggers appear when customers browse specific product pages.

FAQ Matching

Common questions get instant answers from a pre-built FAQ database — no AI inference needed. Faster response, zero cost.

Cart Awareness

Mini Mel knows what's in the customer's cart and makes relevant suggestions based on what they're already buying.

Synonym Recognition

"bracelet" = "bangle" = "wrist piece" — the AI understands product name variations and maps them correctly.

Voice Consistency

Auto-checks every response against Melinda's authentic voice profile. Rewrites anything that sounds generic before the customer sees it.

05 — Security

Enterprise-grade protection for a one-person business.

For non-technical readers: the site protects itself. Spam is blocked invisibly, payments are handled securely by Stripe, and no customer data is exposed. For technical readers: here's the full security stack.

Protection What It Stops
Cloudflare TurnstileSpam bots — invisible challenge, no CAPTCHAs, zero friction
KV-Backed Rate LimitingRequest flooding — sliding window counters with automatic cooldown
Honeypot + Input ValidationAutomated spam submissions and cross-site scripting (XSS)
Timing-Safe AuthenticationPassword guessing — constant-time comparison prevents timing leaks
CSRF Token ProtectionCross-site request forgery — every form verified with unique tokens
Stripe PCI ComplianceCard data never touches the server — tokenised directly to Stripe
